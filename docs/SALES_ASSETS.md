## 3. FAQ

**Q: Is this AI replacing my employees?**
A: No. It's giving your employees superpowers. Your team supervises the AI in Mission Control. Less busywork, more high-value decisions.

**Q: How long does setup take?**
A: Under 1 hour. Our wizard creates your workspace, provisions agents, installs skills, and walks you through your first task.

**Q: What if the AI makes a mistake?**
A: Quality gates. Every task goes through review checkpoints. Nothing reaches owners, tenants, or vendors without human approval.

**Q: Can I try before committing?**
A: Yes. 30-day pilot at discounted rates. Full access. Weekly optimization. Before/after ROI report.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Multi-tenant isolation from day one. Separate workspace, separate database, separate state. Zero cross-contamination.

**Q: What happens if I run out of credits?**
A: Your agents pause. Nothing breaks. Buy more credits anytime from the billing dashboard.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Always. Full data portability. Workspace data, audit logs, task history, billing records.

**Q: Do I need to install anything?**
A: No. Fully hosted. We handle deployment, uptime, security, backups. You just log in.
