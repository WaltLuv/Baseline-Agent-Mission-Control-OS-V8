# Frequently Asked Questions

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Each workspace is isolated. Memory connectors honor source permissions, and Operator Memory never leaves your workspace.

**What happens if I run out of credits?**
Your workforce pauses gracefully. No tasks are lost; runtimes resume when credits are topped up.

**Can I run Mission Control on my own server?**
Yes. Mission Control is self-hostable. See the Deployment guide.

**Do I have to be technical to use this?**
No. The business owner experience is plain-English. Technical setup is only needed for runtimes and integrations.

**How is this different from a chatbot?**
A chatbot answers questions. An AI Employee does work, remembers decisions, asks for approval, and is measured on outcomes.

**Why are some skills "warning" or "inactive"?**
Warning means installed but unused; inactive means unused for 14+ days. Attach the skill to an employee or remove it.

**Why are there citations on every answer?**
So your decisions are informed, not opaque. Click any citation to open the source.

**Can I see what an AI Employee did last week?**
Yes. Open the employee and view its trace. The audit trail covers every action, approval, and outcome.

**How do I switch between Demo and Live?**
Toggle is in the header. The badge always tells you which mode you are in.
