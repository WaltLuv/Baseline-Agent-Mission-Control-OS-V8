## 2. Sales sheet

Solo and small-firm attorneys lose two things to admin: hours that
should be billable, and clients who get a fast response from a
faster competitor.

Baseline deploys four AI employees:

- **AI Intake Coordinator** picks up every inbound 24/7. Runs the
  conflict check against your matter database. Qualifies the
  matter (your practice areas only). Books the consult. Hands you
  a brief.
- **AI Matter Manager** keeps every active matter current. Court
  deadlines, doc requests, status nudges. The bar-grievance fuel
  source ("never heard back") is solved.
- **AI Client Comms** drafts client-facing updates from matter
  notes. You review and send. The 30 minutes a week you spent
  drafting status emails is back.
- **AI Filing Watch** reads the case schedule and flags every
  approaching deadline. Federal + state rules indexed.

Mission Control supervises. Every client communication waits for
attorney approval. Every memory call cites the case file, the
engagement letter, or the rule of procedure.

**Trial:** $1 / 14 days. **Tier:** $299/mo solo · $799/mo firm.

## 3. Discovery script

1. How many attorneys + paralegals on the team?
2. What practice areas? (Litigation, transactional, family, etc.)
3. How are inbound consults handled today — answering service, paralegal, you?
4. What's your average billable hour rate?
5. Active matters?
6. Practice management software — Clio, MyCase, PracticePanther, none?
7. How are you running conflict checks today?
8. Last client who left, and the stated reason?
9. How often do you send a client a status update unprompted?
10. $1, 14-day pilot on 10 active matters + your inbound number?

## 4. Objection handling

| Objection | Response | Proof |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| "Bar rules — AI can't give legal advice." | "AI never gives advice. AI drafts; attorney reviews and signs. Same posture as paralegal work. Audit log per touch." | Show approval queue + audit log |
| "Conflict checks are sacred." | "AI Intake Coordinator runs the check against your database. If it flags a conflict, the consult never books. If it's clean, attorney still confirms. Belt + suspenders." | Show conflict check flow |
| "Clio handles this." | "Clio is the system of record. Mission Control is the workforce that *uses* Clio. Different job." | Demo integration |
| "Clients want me, not AI." | "Clients want a returned call within an hour. AI returns it in 4 minutes, books the consult with you. Same satisfaction in pilot data." | Sample voice flow |
| "Solo practice — can't afford this." | "$299/mo. If it surfaces one missed deadline a year, it pays for itself 10×." | Pricing |
| "Data — privileged communications." | "Encryption in transit + at rest. Audit log. Memory citations cited inside the firm only. SOC 2 path active." | Security doc |
