## 2. Sales sheet

A missed call is a missed job. A missed job is $300–$2,000 of
revenue. Most home-service shops lose 15–40% of inbound calls to
voicemail, after-hours, or "we're busy right now."

Baseline solves it with four AI employees:

- **AI Intake Receptionist** answers every call. Qualifies. Books.
  Hands off to a human if needed. Voice-first, SMS fallback,
  24/7.
- **AI Dispatcher** routes same-day. Knows your crew availability,
  your service area, your SLA windows. Surfaces the emergency
  before you ask.
- **AI Quote Recovery** reactivates the quotes that went cold.
  4-touch cadence, opt-out-clean, hands off to a closer when warm.
- **AI Review Collector** asks every customer after the job. Routes
  5-star to Google. Routes the 1-star straight to you, with the
  job context attached.

Mission Control supervises. Every quote, every reschedule, every
escalation waits for the operator's approval. Memory backed.

**Trial:** $1 / 14 days. **Tier:** $299/mo.

## 3. Discovery script

1. How many inbound calls do you receive per week? (Sizing)
2. What % go to voicemail? Honest number. (15–40% is industry norm)
3. After-hours coverage — voicemail, answering service, or nothing?
4. What's your average job ticket value?
5. How many same-day requests do you get per week, and what % do you fulfill?
6. How are you collecting reviews today? (None / manual / paid service)
7. What software runs your dispatch? (ServiceTitan / Jobber / Housecall Pro / none)
8. Last week, did you miss a call you wish you hadn't? How much was the job worth?
9. If you had a 24/7 receptionist that booked appointments and never asked for a raise, would you keep them?
10. $1 pilot for 14 days — open to it?
