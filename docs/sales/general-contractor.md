## 2. Sales sheet (~250 words)

General contractors lose deals two ways: estimates take too long, or
sub coordination falls apart mid-project. Both are coordination
problems, not skill problems.

Baseline deploys four AI employees:

- **AI Bid Estimator** drafts the estimate from your plans, your
  past bids, and live material pricing. Margin is checked before
  it hits your inbox. Same-day estimates are normal.
- **AI Sub Coordinator** runs the daily status pulse. Who's on-site,
  who's late, who's blocked, who needs a PO. You get one digest,
  not 14 phone calls.
- **AI Schedule Planner** keeps the project plan current. When a
  trade slips, it re-flows the downstream dependencies and surfaces
  the new critical path.
- **AI Permit Tracker** files, tracks, and preps inspections per
  jurisdiction. No more "I thought you pulled it."

Mission Control supervises. Every estimate, every change-order, every
permit submission waits for your approval. Every AI action cites the
doc it came from.

**Trial:** $1 / 14 days. **Tier:** $299/mo. **Demo:**
`mission.baselineautomations.com/?demo=gc`.

## 3. Discovery script

1. How many active projects are you running right now?
2. How long does it take you to turn a request into a signed bid? (4–7 days is normal — anything more is acute pain)
3. Who answers the phone when a sub is late?
4. Walk me through the last project where coordination broke. What happened, what did it cost?
5. What do you use today — Buildertrend, ProCore, CoConstruct, none?
6. Are you doing change orders by email or in a system?
7. How often do permits surprise you?
8. What's your margin on the average $200k job, and where do you lose points?
9. If you had a sub coordinator running 24/7 for $299/mo, would you keep them?
10. If we ran your last completed project through Mission Control in a 14-day pilot for $1, would you want to see the result?

## 4. Objection handling

| Objection | Response | Proof |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| "We already use Buildertrend / ProCore." | "Keep using it. Mission Control sits on top and runs the workforce that updates it." | Demo the integration |
| "Subs don't text — they call." | "AI Sub Coordinator calls them. Voice-first. If they prefer SMS, it shifts. The data lands in your dashboard either way." | Demo the voice flow |
| "Estimates need a human." | "Estimates need a human approver. Drafting them is a $30/hr job. AI Bid Estimator drafts; you approve. You spend 5 minutes instead of 90." | Show the draft + approve flow |
| "I tried AI — it made up materials." | "Memory citations. Every line item points to your bid history or the current material feed. If it's wrong, you see why before you send." | Open a memory citation card |
| "I'm too small for this." | "Smaller crews benefit more, not less. You're the dispatcher, the estimator, and the owner. Pick one to offload first." | Pricing page |
| "Permits are state-specific — you can't know them." | "We index the jurisdiction rules you feed us. Memory-backed. If you operate in Cook County, AI Permit Tracker reads Cook County rules." | Show the permit memory |
