## 2. Sales sheet

The most expensive thing in real estate is a lead that gets a
response in 24 hours. By then they've already booked a showing with
someone faster.

Baseline deploys four AI employees:

- **AI Lead Capture** responds in under 5 minutes, qualifies on
  budget + timeline + area, books the showing, and hands the agent
  a brief. From inbound to booked, no agent touch required.
- **AI Transaction Coordinator** runs every active contract.
  Deadlines, doc chases, state form prep, lender follow-ups. The
  TC role for $299/mo.
- **AI Listing Specialist** drafts the CMA, surfaces pricing
  strategy from recent comps, and prepares the listing brief.
- **AI Nurture Concierge** keeps every cold lead warm with a drip
  that adapts to their behavior. 30-day, 90-day, "buy in spring"
  cadences.

Mission Control supervises. Every contract action waits for the
agent. Every memory call cites the listing or the contract.

**Trial:** $1 / 14 days. **Tier:** $299/mo Team or $799/mo
Workspace for brokerages.

## 3. Discovery script

1. How many active listings + buyers are you / your team running?
2. What's your lead source mix — Zillow, IDX, referral, social?
3. Average response time to an inbound lead today? (Honest)
4. Who handles transaction coordination — staff TC, you, contract TC service?
5. How many contracts are active right now?
6. What's your average GCI per closed deal?
7. What % of leads do you close? (Industry: 2–4%)
8. What software do you run — Follow Up Boss, kvCORE, Sierra, Boomtown, none?
9. Last lead you lost to a faster competitor — what was the property?
10. $1 pilot for 14 days — open?
