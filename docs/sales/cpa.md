## 2. Sales sheet

Tax season doesn't break firms because the work is hard. It breaks
firms because of coordination: 200 clients, each missing 3 docs,
each blocking a return.

Baseline deploys four AI employees:

- **AI Client Intake** runs onboarding. Engagement letter, doc
  checklist, prior-year request — all sent and tracked the same
  day the client signs.
- **AI Doc Chase** runs the missing-doc cadence. Adaptive: text
  → email → call. State-specific language. Pilot data: 6 days
  → 24 hours.
- **AI Reconciliation Watch** reads the bookkeeping ledger as it
  comes in and surfaces discrepancies before they hit your desk.
  Client #88's 1099 mismatch is yours to approve, not yours to
  find.
- **AI Review Prep** assembles returns. Partner sees a stack
  ready for review, not a stack of half-built files.

Mission Control supervises. Every client communication waits for
the partner's approval. Every memory call cites the engagement
letter or the bookkeeping line.

**Trial:** $1 / 14 days. **Tier:** $299/mo (single partner) /
$799/mo (multi-partner). Independent of CPA practice software.

## 3. Discovery script

1. How many active 1040 / 1120 / 1065 clients does the firm carry?
2. How many partners + staff are on the team?
3. Tax season pain — quantify in hours per partner per week from Feb 15 to April 15.
4. Who chases missing docs today?
5. What's your current practice software? (UltraTax, Lacerte, Drake, CCH, etc.)
6. What's your bookkeeping stack? (QBO, Xero, Sage)
7. How are you doing reconciliation reviews — sample, full, or skip?
8. Last filing that went over deadline — what was the missing piece?
9. What % of clients are on a fixed-fee engagement vs. hourly?
10. $1, 14-day pilot for one staff member on a 20-client subset?

## 4. Objection handling

| Objection | Response | Proof |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| "We're a relationship business." | "AI handles the admin. Partners get more time for the relationship, not less. The client gets faster turnaround." | Show partner-time savings |
| "We can't have AI talking to clients." | "AI drafts; partner approves. Every touch logged. Same compliance posture as your assistant." | Approval queue |
| "UltraTax / Lacerte does this." | "Practice software does the return. We do the coordination *around* the return — intake, doc chase, recon review, partner prep." | Demo the workflow gap |
| "Tax season is once a year — not worth it." | "It's once a year for partners. It's 12 months a year for the staff chasing 1099s and corrected K-1s. Reconciliation Watch runs monthly." | Show year-round value |
| "We tried automation — clients hated it." | "Adaptive cadence with partner-approved copy. No 'press 1 for' loops. We A/B'd this in pilot." | Sample copy |
| "Data sensitivity — IRS, state." | "Encryption in transit + at rest, full audit log per touch, memory citations cite the source doc, SOC 2 path in progress. We will sign a customer-specific addendum for state regulators." | Security doc |
