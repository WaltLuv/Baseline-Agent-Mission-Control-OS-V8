## 2. Sales sheet (~280 words)

Marketing agencies don't have a creative problem. They have a
**throughput problem**. Reports take a day. Content calendars run
empty. Inbound leads sit until Tuesday. Account managers spend 60%
of their week on admin, then bill the client for strategy.

Baseline deploys five AI employees who work alongside the agency
team:

- **AI Campaign Operator** drafts the campaign brief, routes creative
  to the right designer, and runs QA against the brand book before
  anything hits an ad account. Same-day campaign turnaround is normal.
- **AI Content Calendar Manager** keeps the 30-day calendar full.
  Pulls hooks from past winners. Schedules posts. Flags gaps before
  the client does.
- **AI Client Success Assistant** drafts the weekly status, preps the
  meeting deck, writes the recap. The AM opens their laptop and the
  client update is already drafted in their voice.
- **AI Reporting Analyst** pulls from every ad platform you run
  (Google, Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn) and drafts the client ROI report —
  KPIs, deltas, plain-language analysis. Monthly reports go from 4
  hours to 12 minutes of review.
- **AI Lead Follow-Up Agent** runs adaptive cadence on every inbound.
  4 touches over 10 days. Hands off warm leads to the AM.

Mission Control supervises. Every client-facing communication waits
for AM approval. Every memory call cites the brand book, the
performance log, or the engagement letter.

**Trial:** $1 / 14 days on one test client account. **Tier:**
$299/mo Team (single agency, up to 5 client workspaces) /
$799/mo Workspace (up to 25 client workspaces).

## 3. Discovery script (10 questions)

1. How many active client accounts is the agency running?
2. What service lines — paid, SEO, content, social, full-funnel?
3. How long does a weekly client report take an AM to produce today? (Honest hours)
4. Who fills the content calendar? Account manager, content lead, contractor, nobody?
5. What's your average client MRR?
6. What's the AM-to-client ratio today? (Looking for: pain at 1:8 or worse)
7. Inbound lead follow-up — automated, manual, dropped?
8. What ad platforms + tools do you run on? (Google / Meta / TikTok / LinkedIn / HubSpot / etc.)
9. Last client churn — root cause? (Looking for: "communication", "reporting", "they felt ignored")
10. $1, 14-day pilot on one test client account?

Yes to #10 → demo within 48 hours. Anything less → sales sheet
+ recorded demo, follow-up at T+5.

## 4. Objection handling

| Objection | Response | Proof |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| "Our AMs are the relationship — clients want them." | "AI drafts; AM approves and sends in their voice. Clients see the AM, not the AI. The AM gets 4 hours of their week back to actually talk strategy." | Show client-facing draft → AM approval flow |
| "We tried ChatGPT for content. It sounded like ChatGPT." | "AI Content Calendar Manager pulls from the client's brand book and past winners. Voice is memory-cited, not generic. Test it on your worst client and see." | Memory citation card on a content draft |
| "Reporting is custom per client." | "AI Reporting Analyst learns each client's KPI set in week 1. The template is shared; the report is bespoke. Watch a 4-client run side-by-side." | Demo the reporting output |
| "Ad accounts have permission complexity." | "We read; we don't write to ad platforms without approval. AI Reporting Analyst pulls metrics. Campaign changes wait for the AM." | Show the ad-platform read-only mode |
| "We already use Slack + Notion + Asana." | "Keep them. Mission Control sits on top and runs the AI workforce *across* them. We update Notion the way your AM does." | Demo the integration writes |
| "Leads are sales' job, not delivery." | "AI Lead Follow-Up runs the cadence; your sales team closes. Worst case it covers nights and weekends. Pilot data: 18-32% conversion lift on inbound." | Show the cadence + handoff |
| "Multi-client memory — won't they bleed into each other?" | "Per-client workspace ID, per-client memory namespace, per-client approval queue. Audited per touch. One client never sees another." | Show workspace isolation |
| "We're a 3-person agency — too small." | "Smallest agencies benefit fastest. The founder is the AM, the strategist, and the closer. Pick one to offload first." | Pricing + Team plan |
