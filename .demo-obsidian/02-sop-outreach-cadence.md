# SOP — Client outreach cadence (Q1)

> Tags: sop · outreach · cpa
> Source: Walter's playbook, refined this week

## Cadence

| Touch | Channel | Tone | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| T+0 | Email | Warm intro, request docs | AI |
| T+72h | Email | Plain reminder, attach checklist | AI |
| T+7d  | Phone or text | Direct, name the deadline | Walter |
| T+10d | Partner email | "We can't file without these" | Lena |

## What AI must never do

- Send a second reminder if the client has replied within the window.
- Send a deadline email without including the exact form list outstanding.
- Use "kindly" or "please advise" — strikes false notes with our owners.

## What AI should do automatically

- Pre-fill the deadline date based on filing calendar.
- Quote the specific dollar exposure if a deadline is missed.
- Attach the prior-year doc list as a checklist.
